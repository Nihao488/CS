Sichuan Cuisine

KUNG PAO CHICKEN 宫保鸡丁

HOT POT 火锅

MAPO TOFU 麻婆豆腐

  1. Kung Pao Chicken, is a spicy stir-fried Chinese dish made with chicken nuggets, peanuts, chilies, peppercorns, etc. It is a classic dish in Sichuan cuisine, which originated in Sichuan Province, China. This dish tastes spicy, sweet and slightly nutty. The peanuts added to the meal are responsible for the nutty flavor of the dish.
  2. Hot pot is a cooking method that originated in China. The heat source on the table keeps a pot of soup boiling, and there are various Chinese ingredients and ingredients on the side of the pot for diners to put into the hot soup. Hot pot ingredients vary across China. In the Sichuan region, Sichuan peppercorns and hot peppers are used as the main condiments, so hot pot in Sichuan province can be quite spicy.
  3. Mapo Tofu is a popular Chinese dish from Sichuan Province. It consists of tofu and a spicy sauce, usually a thin, rich, bright red chili oil, based on watercress and tempeh, and ground meat.

FUQI FEIPIAN 夫妻肺片

BENBEN CHICKEN 钵钵鸡

TWICE-COOKED PORK 回锅肉

  1. Fuqi Feipian are a famous traditional dish in Sichuan Province. Usually, beef scalp, beef heart, beef tongue, tripe, and beef are used as the main ingredients, marinated, and then sliced. Add chili oil, pepper noodles and other auxiliary materials to make red oil and pour it on top. It is finely made, beautiful in color, tender in quality, fresh in taste, spicy and fragrant, and very palatable.
  2. BenBen Chicken is a traditional Sichuan snack. It is made by mixing boneless chicken slices with a variety of seasonings in an earthenware bowl with spicy and spicy seasoning. It has the characteristics of crispy skin and tender meat, spicy and delicious, moderately sweet and salty. It was once judged by Sichuan people as a high-quality famous snack.
  3. Twice-cooked pork originated in Sichuan. Most families in Sichuan can make it. The so-called Twice-cooked means to cook again. The status of twice-cooked pork in Sichuan cuisine is very important. Twice-cooked pork has always been considered the top of Sichuan cuisine, the incarnation of Sichuan cuisine. When it comes to Sichuan cuisine, twice-cooked pork must be thought of. Twice-cooked meat is delicious in color and fragrance, and it is the dish that most people will choose in the next meal.

Cantonese Cuisine

CANTONESE POACHED CHICKEN
白切鸡

CANTONESE STYLE CRISPY ROAST DUCK
广式脆皮烤鸭

BARBECUED PORK WITH HONEY SAUCE
蜜汁叉烧

  1. Cantonese Poached Chicken is a classic Cantonese dish that is commonly found in southern regions such as Guangdong. Boiled chicken has a beautiful shape, yellow skin and white meat, tender and delicious, and the taste is extremely delicious and very delicious. The flesh is white and buttery, with the smell of scallion oil. It is delicious and has a unique flavor.
  2. Cantonese Style Crispy Roast Duck is a famous traditional dish in Guangdong Province. This dish is made from roasted whole duck. The color of the dish is golden red, the duck body is full, and the belly contains marinade, and the taste is mellow. Fragrant, fat but not greasy. Most people eat it with sour plum sauce, which has a unique flavor.
  3. Barbecued Pork With Honey Sauce is one of the famous traditional dishes in Guangdong Province. It is mainly made of pork (lean), barbecued pork sauce, oyster sauce, garlic, light soy sauce, cooking wine, honey, sugar, and peanut oil.

BRAISED PORK WITH PRESERVED VEGETABLES
梅菜扣肉

RICE NOODLE ROLL
肠粉

GUANGFU SOUP
广府汤

  1. Brasied pork with pickled vegetables is boiled pork belly in a soup pot, added dark soy sauce, deep-fried and colored, and then cut into slices. After that, add seasoning and stir-fry for a while, then put in the soup and simmer over low heat. Put the pork belly into a bowl, put the plum vegetables on top, pour in the original soup and steam it thoroughly. Then turn the meat upside down on a plate. After cooking, the meat is rotten and fragrant, and it tastes salty and slightly sweet, fat but not greasy.
  2. Rice rolls are a very famous snack in Guangdong. There are two types of rice rolls, salty and sweet. The fillings of salty rice rolls are mainly pork, beef, shrimp, pork liver, etc., while the fillings of sweet rice rolls are mainly vegetables and fruits soaked in sugar, and then mixed with fried sesame seeds. Rice rolls are mainly divided into two types according to genres, one is braised rolls, and the other is drawer rolls. Due to the different production tools used, the rolls are different.
  3. Guangfu Soup is a secret recipe of nourishing food and health that has been passed down by Cantonese people for thousands of years. The Cantonese soup cooked in a slow fire has been cooked for a long time. It not only has the effect of medicine and tonic, but also has the sweetness of the mouth. It has a history of thousands of years.

Henan Cuisine

DAOKOU STYLE ROAST CHICKEN
道口烧鸡

MUTTON BRAISED NOODLES
羊肉烩面

NON-STICK EGG PUDDING
三不沾

  1. Daokou roast chicken is one of the characteristic traditional dishes in Henan Province, and is known as the first chicken in China. The production skills of Daokou Roasted Chicken have been passed down from generation to generation, and it has its own unique style. It was once rated as a premium product by the Ministry of Commerce. It is the number one food in the country and is famous overseas.
  2. There is no complicated production process for mutton stewed noodles. Mutton stewed noodles use mutton soup as the base soup, pour the cooked noodles into it, and finally add some ingredients and chili oil. This makes the mutton stewed noodles retain the deliciousness of mutton and has rich nutritional value.
  3. Triple nonstick is made by mixing eggs, starch and sugar with water. It is not only golden in color, but also delicious in taste. The reason why it is called three non-stick is because it does not stick to plates, chopsticks and teeth, so it is called three non-stick. The taste is delicious and nutritious, especially suitable for the elderly and young children.

HULATANG
胡辣汤

SPRING CHICKEN IN SALT
桶子鸡

DRAGON'S BEARD CANDY
龙须糕

  1. Hulatang is a famous traditional soup in Henan breakfast. With its popular taste and low price, Hulatang has always been the first choice for people's breakfast.
  2. Spring Chicken in Salt is made of high-quality fat and tender live hens with a growth period of no more than three years, and is made by simmering in a century-old stewed soup. It is characterized by golden color, fat but not greasy, tender and crisp, and mellow taste.
  3. Dragon's Beard Candy is a flavor dessert and also a delicious delicacy. It is made of rice and flour. It has the characteristics of beautiful color, sweet and salty taste, and unique flavor. The surface of the cake is whisker-shaped, crispy and soft. It is The most popular delicacy among desserts is also a snack for satisfying cravings, and its production process is also very unique.
